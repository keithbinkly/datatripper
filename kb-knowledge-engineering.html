Making enterprise data AI-ready through context, semantics, and governance

AI systems promise revolutionary conversational access to data, but most projects fail because corporate data lacks the necessary business context and semantic clarity. Traditional data pipelines were designed for transactional processing and reporting, often decoupling data from its business meaning‚Äîcreating a critical AI Context Gap.

Key Terms

AI Context Gap
The space between model context and organizational context that humans know but haven't documented.
Action-Ready Data
Data embedding business context required for automated decision-making by AI systems.
Context Engineering
Engineering explicit knowledge into AI systems to close the AI Context Gap.
Semantic Layer
The authoritative source of business context that persists across system transformations.
Ontology
A formal, machine-readable specification defining entities, relationships, and constraints.
Controlled Vocabulary
A curated list of approved terms‚Äîthe smallest unit of semantic governance.

Why Now

95% of AI projects stall due to data readiness issues. Legacy data quality (60-70%) is far below the 99%+ required for production AI. Context engineering provides semantic rails to guide AI from probabilistic inference to verifiable knowledge.
