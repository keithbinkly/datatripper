◈ KEY TERMS
AI Context Gap Space between what AI knows and what humans know but haven't documented—95% of AI projects fail here
Semantic Layer Authoritative source of business meaning that persists across system transformations
Controlled Vocabulary Curated list of approved terms with one meaning each—the smallest unit of semantic governance
Ontology Machine-readable specification defining entities, relationships, and constraints for automated inference
Context Engineering Discipline of engineering explicit knowledge into AI systems to close the Context Gap
Action-Ready Data Data with embedded business context and rules required for automated AI decision-making
Semantic Ambiguity Data stripped of business context becomes meaningless to AI—does "A" mean Active or Approved?
